Elderflower Cosmopolitan

1.5 oz Vodka
1 oz Cointreau
1 oz Saint Germain
1 oz Cranberry Juice (If available)
1/2 oz Lime Juice (If available)
Ice cubes
Lime twist for garnish
Combine all ingredients in a shaker filled with ice. Shake well and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a lime twist.
